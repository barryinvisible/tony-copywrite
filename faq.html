What is copywriting? Do I need a copywriter?
Only you can answer these questions. Good copywriting can help you educate, inform, inspire and attract clients. Is style, image and presentation important to you? Do you want to engage and attract your audience intellectually and emotionally? Do you want to grow your business and increase your sales? If you answered ‘yes’ to these questions, then you need a copywriter.
What does the copywriting process involve?
Brief Tell me everything about your business, your clients, your audience and what you are trying to say. Proposal I will send you through a proposal of how I can help. Questions You will then get a copywriting brief, asking for more detailed information. I will also do my own research. Draft This is just the start of the conversation. You will receive a copy of my first draft and after that, we can discuss, review, revise or even take an axe to it. You should read it carefully to see if I have understood your product, business, client, target audience and message. Is the language, style, tone of voice, spelling and grammar correct? Let’s do a feedback session and we can tweak, polish and perfect your message. I will then submit the final version.
How long does it take?
Depends on size and complexity, but I will meet an agreed deadline. Be careful with last minute stuff, we should never compromise on quality
What if I am not happy with the first draft?
Nobody is. The first draft is always the start of the conversation.
