A famous Russian man once said, ‘Let me speak from my heart in English’. A not very famous Englishman has a message for you: Don’t even think about translating your marketing materials from Russian into English, for the love of god, do not do it! Products and services don’t always sell themselves. That’s where I come in. With a fresh set of eyes, I’ll let you know quickly if I can help build you a bigger stage, put you in the spotlight and give you a louder voice. An Englishman on a world tour in 1998, I only came to Russia for 6 months. Here I am, 22 years later, still in Moscow and counting. All that time spent working with the big boys in the Russian business world. Helping them to say things clearer, better, louder and more persuasively. To grow your business you need to attract, engage and persuade your audience both intellectually and emotionally. Let’s see if I can help
